Green Eggs And Ham

Back

Description

Green Eggs and Ham is a very popelar dish that brings joy to children who know the Dr.Suess classic story

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Ham
  • Green Dye

Steps

  1. Add green dye to ham before oven
  2. Grab a pan and stick your ham in the oven
  3. Beat eggs till they are scrambled and add green dye
  4. Cook eggs in pan
  5. Last step is to enjoy with a smile on your face
